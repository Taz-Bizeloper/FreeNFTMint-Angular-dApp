How It Works

The minted NFT will be an ERC-1155 token that lives on the Ethereum blockchain. You can then use it as your Twitter NFT profile picture, transfer it to a friend/family member, add images to your NFT photo frame, or list it for sale on an NFT marketplace.

Step 1

Connect your wallet to the Ethereum Mainnet and connect it to the site

Step 2

Upload an image (.jpg .png or .gif) from your device. The image will be stored onto IPFS decentralized servers where it will never get removed

Step 3

Give your image a name and a description. This will be public information added with your image that everyone can see

Step 4

Enter the quantity of the image to mint. You can multiple copies of the same NFT that you can own, sell, or transfer

Step 5

Agree to the terms and click the "Mint Image" button. This will start the upload process to IPFS and then trigger the mint transaction to your connected wallet App

Step 6

Open your wallet App that you connected with to confirm and complete the mint transaction. You will have to pay the gas fees to mint

Step 7

Find the NFT in your wallet
